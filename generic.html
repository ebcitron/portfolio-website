What and who am I?

Not an easy question to answer. I like to think of myself as Millenial Rennesance Man.. ( Which sounds like an Oxymoron) I was gifted/cursed with a brain that never stops thinking things through, questioning them, and seeking for answers. I am constantly reading and teaching myself New things and often find myself down the rabbit hole, having read for hours, with no real end goal. I was born to be a computer programmer, it just took me a decade to make the final decision to make a career of it. The past 10 years I have been to school, worked 5 Jobs, University, taken on many projects, and learned alot in life. I and others feel that I have accumulated more life Experience than most people my age.
